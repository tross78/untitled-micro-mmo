# Phase 8.9c — First-10-Minute Onboarding Pass

Sourced from the gameplay audit (2026-05-15). Items below are confirmed issues deferred from the 8.78 fix pass.

## Onboarding

- **First-session cellar guard auto-prompt** — nothing forces a new player to interact with the cellar guard; they can wander indefinitely with no instruction. Fix: auto-open the guard's dialogue on first spawn (one-time flag in localStorage). Minimal change agreed; deferred here for the full onboarding pass.
- **forestFights explanation** — `⚡N` appears in the HUD with no tooltip or tutorial text. New players don't know what it means or that it resets daily. Fix: add a one-time explanatory log line on first combat attempt, and a tooltip or legend entry in the controls menu.
- **Threat level scale** — `☠️3` is shown with no maximum, so players can't judge urgency. Fix: show as `☠️3/5` or add a legend entry.

## Content Gaps (defer to content pass if not done here)

- **Dead-end rooms need payoff** — library, watchtower, frozen_lake, and smuggler_den are single-exit rooms with no enemy, no quest hook, and no unique mechanic. Players who explore these feel cheated. Fix: add at least one of: a unique item pickup, a lore scroll, a fishing/gathering node, or a side quest hook.
- **mountain_trial narrative placement** — the barkeep quest is gated by the militia chain (`cave_troll_bounty`) but lives in the barkeep chain, which is narratively awkward. Fix: foreshadow it clearly in barkeep's base dialogue, or move it into the militia chain.
- **ancient_throne prerequisites** — `mountain_trial` is not required for `ancient_throne` despite the layout implying the mountain route matters. Fix: tighten the intended endgame prerequisite story.
- **Catacombs dual-exit signage** — the catacombs has both `up` (ruins_descent) and `north` (cemetery) exits on the same side, which is mentally confusing. Fix: separate those routes spatially or differentiate their in-room signage.
